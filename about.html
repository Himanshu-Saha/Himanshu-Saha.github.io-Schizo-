Artist Profile

Growing up. Prakhar Verma was mesmerized by the scintillating landscapes of his homeland Uttarakhand. State's natural beauty & serenity have always been the inspiration of his spintuality and artistic forms.

Post graduation, he decided that he wanted to explore more of his hand work sketches and tattoos rather than working in an advertising agency. After completing his B.Sc in Multimedia: Animation, Prakhar quit 3 jobs and started as a full time tattoo artist.

He concocted his spiritual self to the dark and alternative aesthetics creating a personal touch in his art to establish authenticity.

Whole process of making an art is meditative for an artist. He is intrigued with preconceived concepts as well as instinctive decisions which reflect in his visual language.
